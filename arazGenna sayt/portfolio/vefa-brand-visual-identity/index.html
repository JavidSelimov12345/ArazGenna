VEFA

Brand Name, Brand Visual Identity

 

We prepared a visual brand identity for Vefa Holding, the leading brand in prefabricated construction systems, using graphics that perfectly represent the Holding’s area of activity and corporate culture. We reflected Vefa’s multi-branded structure with the letter “V,” which we formed with circles in two different colors. Additionally, these circles illustrate the ability of Vefa and its subsidiaries to produce global products and services. The primary colors we used to demonstrate the brand identity represent Vefa’s business segment and enhances its position as a leading brand.
